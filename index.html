Our Heritage

The Art of Fine Weaving

Shahi Weaves started with a simple appreciation for traditional Indian textiles. We work directly with weavers to source real Lucknowi Chikankari, traditional block prints, and hand-woven silks. Every piece is curated to represent authentic craft and classic styles.

Our name represents this focus on premium materials ("Shahi") and the craft itself. We select fabrics and prints that wear comfortably while looking dignified.

Frequently Asked Questions

Every outfit is crafted using traditional handloom techniques passed down through generations. We source pure mulberry silk, organic cotton, and hand-spun linen directly from artisan weavers across Lucknow, Banaras, and Jaipur, preserving Indian heritage.

Once you request a consultation, our lead designer coordinates a virtual session to guide you through taking measurements. We then hand-weave and tailor the outfit custom to your silhouette.

We offer free insured shipping across India and international shipping options. Since each outfit is tailored or hand-finished, we do not accept standard returns but provide complimentary alterations to guarantee a perfect fit.

We recommend professional dry cleaning for all silk, velvet, and heavily embroidered Anarkali pieces. For lighter cotton and linen collections, gentle hand washing with mild detergents in cold water is suitable.

Discounts are applied automatically by collection tier: 25% off on Daily Rhythm, 20% off on Festive Radiant, and 10% off on Glam Soul. Struck-through values represent the original artisan base price.
