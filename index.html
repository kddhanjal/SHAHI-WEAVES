Our Heritage

The Art of Fine Weaving

Shahi Weaves started with a simple appreciation for traditional Indian textiles. We work directly with weavers to source real Lucknowi Chikankari, traditional block prints, and hand-woven silks. Every piece is curated to represent authentic craft and classic styles.

Our name represents this focus on premium materials ("Shahi") and the craft itself. We select fabrics and prints that wear comfortably while looking dignified.

Frequently Asked Questions

We source pure silk, organic cotton, and handloom linen directly from local weavers in Lucknow, Banaras, and Jaipur. All outfits are made using traditional hand-weaving methods to keep these historic Indian crafts alive.

After you submit the custom request form, our lead designer will schedule a video call with you. We will walk you through taking your measurements step-by-step and then tailor the outfit to fit your specific size.

We offer free shipping all over India and standard international shipping. Because our clothes are custom-tailored or hand-finished for each customer, we do not accept regular returns. However, we offer free sizing alterations to make sure your outfit fits you perfectly.

We advise dry cleaning for all silk, velvet, and heavy Anarkali suits. For our light cotton or linen everyday wear, you can gently hand wash them in cold water with a mild soap.

Discounts are automatically added based on the collection: 25% off for Daily Rhythm, 20% off for Festive Radiant, and 10% off for Glam Soul. The crossed-out prices show the original retail value.
